Individual

As an individual you can partner with our organisation through offerring any of you donations. We are well supported by various organisation in this events. By simple registration on our platform you get to apply for transplants and blood donations in any of our branches.You can also visit some of our branches and register yourself for donation

Corporate company

There is also an opportunity companies as one can offer to donate organs. One can register themselves as an organisation to donate blood and organs as a unit oneline. The CEO or the general manager is expected to fill the application form oneline and submit it. Also mydonor foundation provides a opportunity to host a blood drive inorder to donate certain body organs.

Hospital

our platform offers hospitals a chance to register on our site and advertise organ donations under the foundation contracts so as to help reach out to our goal.
