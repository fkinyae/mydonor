ABOUT US

History

MYDONOR is an organization established in 5th June 2005 in Nairobi,Kenya.

Our aim is to donate blood and internal body organs to those that have experienced body organ failure or have lost a lot of blood and require high medical attention.

The government along with other institutions like hospitals within the country have partnered with our organization inorder to donate blood and certain body organs to recipients within the country who urgently need it.

Over the past 16 years, we have been focusing our collective capabilities on ensuring that those who need to be donated urgently, we connect them to various donors who are willing to help inorder to uplift their suffering and save their lives.

In the next 3 years,we aim to have reached out to over a thousand recipients and help solve their lives.

Challenges faced

  • Finding organ donors has been a major challenge.
  • At times we are also unable to meet the high demand due to lack of enough donors.
  • Funds have also been a challenge since without the necessary funds,we are unable to keep the organization running well.

Our vision

To build a platform where donors are able to communicate to us inorder to donate various recipients.

Our core values

Integrity

We shall be honest, accountable and transparent with the blood and various body organs we receive for the operation and safeguard the integrity of those that have donated.

Service to humanity

We embody responsibility, accountability and commitment and we faithfully carry out those duties that we have been trusted upon and maintain the highest ethical and professional humanitarian standards.

Respect

We serve with respect, honouring the people we serve, our partners, the communities and one another.We value the relationships we build with our communities and one another.

Synergy

We pursue open communication and practice teamwork to achieve our goals.

Excellence

We pursue superior performance because someone's life depends on it.
