The Challenge


Expand an existing grantmaking program internationally



Ford Motor Company is a global automotive industry leader with facilities across six continents. In 2014, Ford was recognized as one of the world's most ethical companies by Ethisphere for the fifth year in a row. Ford has also been named as one of Corporate Social Responsibility magazine's 100 Best Corporate Citizens in the U.S.

As part of its corporate social responsibility initiatives, the Ford Motor Company and Ford Fund provide more than $37M annually in grants to nonprofit organizations. GlobalGiving's partnership with Ford began in 2007 with its Brazil program, for which GlobalGiving identified and made grants to local partners. By 2012, the company was looking to expand its giving programs, as it operates in 77 locations worldwide.Ford needed a grantmaking program that could:
  • Increase Ford's international social impact;
  • Identify potential grantees abroad;
  • Ensure trustworthiness of local grantee organizations;
  • Facilitate communication with grantees; and
  • Quantify the impacts of giving.
